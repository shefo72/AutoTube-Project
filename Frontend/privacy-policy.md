# AutoTube Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 2026

## Your Privacy Matters

At AutoTube, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered YouTube automation platform.

## What Information Do We Collect?

To provide you with accurate analytics and tailored content generation, we collect the following data:

- **Account Information:** Your name and email address when you register.
- **YouTube Data:** When you securely connect your YouTube channel, we access your video metrics, channel statistics, and public content to perform niche analysis and content gap research.

## How Do We Use Your Data?

Our primary goal is to help you grow your YouTube channel. We only use your data to:

- Analyze your channel's performance and identify ranking opportunities.
- Generate customized video scripts, titles, descriptions, and thumbnail hooks.
- Display your analytics directly within the AutoTube dashboard.

## Third-Party Services & AI Providers

AutoTube connects with trusted third-party services to function. By using AutoTube, you also interact with these services:

- **YouTube API Services:** We use YouTube API Services to fetch your channel metrics. By using our platform, you agree to be bound by the [YouTube Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms) and the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **AI Generation Models:** We securely send contextual prompts to AI providers (such as OpenAI and Google Gemini) to generate your scripts and text. We **do not** share your personal account credentials with these AI models.

## Keeping Your Data Secure

We implement strict security measures to protect your information.

- We use standard **OAuth 2.0** for all YouTube connections.
- **We never see, collect, or store your Google password.**
- All communication between your browser and our servers is encrypted.

## Your Privacy Rights & Revoking Access

You have full control over your data. You can request to delete your AutoTube account at any time.

Additionally, you can revoke AutoTube's access to your YouTube channel at any moment by visiting your [Google Security Settings page](https://security.google.com/settings/security/permissions).

## Updates to this Policy

We may update this policy periodically as we add new features to the platform. If significant changes are made, we will notify you via the email associated with your account.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy or your data, please open an issue in our GitHub repository or contact the development team.
